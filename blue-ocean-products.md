# Blue-Ocean Product Brainstorm

> 7 alternative ideas outside the Content Formula / Bastion / Works With Agents orbit.
> Scored on: moat, speed-to-revenue, unfair advantage match, and £55K→£80K+ trajectory.

---

## Scoring System

- **Moat** (1-10): How hard to copy? Defensibility.
- **Speed** (1-10): Weeks to first pound of revenue.
- **Advantage** (1-10): How well does it leverage Vilius' specific skills?
- **Revenue ceiling** (1-10): How big can this get?

---

## 1. 🔒 Local-First AI Desktop App

**What:** Electron app running Bonsai 1-bit models locally. "ChatGPT, but private." Drag-drop documents, chat, summarize, translate — all offline.

**Why blue ocean:** Every AI app sends data to OpenAI/Anthropic. Nobody ships a polished consumer local AI app. LM Studio is dev-tool. Ollama is CLI-first. The consumer space is empty.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 4 | 7 | 8 | 7 | **26/40** |

**Revenue model:** Free tier (1.7B model) + Pro (£8/mo, 8B model + document Q&A). 1,000 Pro users = £96K ARR.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐ — Good consumer play, but distribution is hard. Better as Bastion's marketing funnel.

---

## 2. 🏥 NHS Compliance AI Scanner

**What:** SaaS that scans NHS organizations' policies, DSP submissions, and IG documentation for gaps, inconsistencies, and missing evidence. Produces a compliance score + remediation plan.

**Why blue ocean:** NHS DSP/IG compliance is mandatory, expensive, and currently done manually by overworked IG teams. 219 NHS trusts × £5K/year = £1.1M TAM just in England. Add private healthcare, social care, local authorities.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 9 | 5 | 9 | 8 | **31/40** |

**Revenue model:** £499/mo per trust. 20 trusts = £120K ARR. Procurement cycle is the bottleneck, not willingness to pay.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Highest moat + advantage combo. NHS expertise is rare. Compliance is non-discretionary spend.

## 3. 🎛️ AI Agent Pipeline Marketplace

**What:** A marketplace of pre-built AI agent workflows. Like Zapier templates but for autonomous agents. "One-click deploy an agent that monitors your GitHub, writes weekly reports, and posts to Slack."

**Why blue ocean:** Everyone's building agents. Nobody's selling pre-configured agent workflows. The "app store for agents" doesn't exist yet.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 5 | 6 | 9 | 6 | **26/40** |

**Revenue model:** Marketplace take rate (30%). Free workflows + premium (£10-50 each). Network effects if it takes off.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐ — High advantage match but requires two-sided marketplace. Chicken-and-egg risk.

---

## 4. 📋 Compliance Document Auto-Writer

**What:** AI that writes DPIAs, FOI responses, SAR responses, Equality Impact Assessments from templates. You describe the project → AI writes the compliance document. "TurboTax for compliance paperwork."

**Why blue ocean:** Every NHS project needs a DPIA. Every FOI response takes hours. SAR responses are legally time-bound. Nobody automates this end-to-end. Existing tools (OneTrust) are enterprise-only and ££££.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 7 | 8 | 8 | 8 | **31/40** |

**Revenue model:** SaaS — £49/mo (individual), £199/mo (team), £499/mo (organization). 50 orgs = £300K ARR.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Fastest to revenue. Clear pain point. Templates already exist in Bastion DPIA/FOI/SAR web parts. Extract, wrap, sell.

## 5. 📊 Local LLM Benchmarking Service

**What:** SaaS that tests local LLMs (oMLX, llama.cpp, Ollama, Bonsai) on YOUR specific use case. Upload your prompts → see which model performs best → get deployment config. "Which local LLM should I run for my NHS document summarization?"

**Why blue ocean:** Local LLM adoption is exploding. Everyone asks "which model?" Nobody answers quantitatively for specific use cases. LMSys Chatbot Arena is general-purpose. This is domain-specific.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 3 | 4 | 7 | 4 | **18/40** |

**Revenue model:** Freemium — 5 free benchmarks/month, Pro £29/mo for unlimited. Niche market.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐ — Interesting but too niche. Better as a feature of something else.

---

## 6. 🏢 "AI Readiness Audit" SaaS

**What:** A self-serve audit that scores an organization's readiness for AI adoption. Covers compliance (GDPR, DSP, ISO), infrastructure (do you have GPUs?), skills (who knows what?), data (is your data AI-ready?), and governance (who approves AI use?).

**Why blue ocean:** Every board is asking "are we ready for AI?" Nobody has a structured answer. Consultants charge £10K+ for this. Automate the 80%.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 5 | 7 | 8 | 7 | **27/40** |

**Revenue model:** £499 per audit report. 100 audits/year = £50K. Add consulting upsells at £2K/day.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Great consulting funnel. Low technical barrier. Directly leads to Bastion sales.

---

## 7. 🧠 Personal AI Memory System

**What:** A productized version of Honcho/Hermes memory — personal knowledge graph + local AI that remembers everything you tell it across all your tools. "Second brain that actually works."

**Why blue ocean:** Notion/Obsidian are manual. AI memory tools (Rewind, Limitless) are surveillance-heavy. Nobody does private, local, agent-integrated memory well.

| Moat | Speed | Advantage | Revenue | **Total** |
|------|-------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 6 | 3 | 6 | 8 | **23/40** |

**Revenue model:** Consumer SaaS — £5/mo individual, team pricing TBD. High volume needed.

**Verdict:** ⭐⭐⭐ — Massive market but hard execution. Long build time. Better as a Bastion feature.

## Recommendation

**Build #1 first: Compliance Document Auto-Writer.** Fastest to revenue, directly monetizes existing Bastion DPIA/FOI/SAR templates, solves a clear mandatory pain point. Extract the compliance web parts from Bastion, wrap as standalone SaaS, add pricing page, launch.

**If that works → build #2 (NHS Compliance Scanner)** as the upsell. Scanner audits → Auto-Writer fixes. Natural funnel.

Both products share the same core differentiator: *"We understand regulated industries because we work in them. Not Silicon Valley guessing."*
