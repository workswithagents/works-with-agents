## What Happens After Filing

1. **Same day:** Company number issued via email
2. **24 hours:** Company appears on Companies House register
3. **1-2 weeks:** Certificate of incorporation arrives by post
4. **Within 3 months:** Register for Corporation Tax (HMRC will send you a letter)

---

## Post-Incorporation Checklist

- [ ] Open business bank account (Starling, Tide, Monzo Business — free)
- [ ] Register for VAT if turnover > £90K (not immediately)
- [ ] Set up accounting software (FreeAgent — free with Mettle/NatWest)
- [ ] Register domain name: `bastion.uk` or `workswithagents.com`
- [ ] Set up Stripe account using company details
- [ ] Get professional indemnity insurance (~£200/year with Hiscox or PolicyBee)
- [ ] Set up Confirmation Statement reminder (due 12 months after incorporation)
