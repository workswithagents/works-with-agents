Questions You Might Have

Does this work with any AI agent?

Yes. The methodology is agent-agnostic. It works with Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, Cursor, Devin — any AI coding agent. The patterns are universal.

I'm a solo developer. Is this for me?

Absolutely. The self-paced course is designed for individual developers who want to 10x their output. The methodology was developed by a solo developer working nights and weekends.

What if agents improve and this becomes obsolete?

The methodology is about principles, not tool-specific tricks. Agents will get better, but the patterns — memory, skills, autonomy, quality gates — are fundamental to human-AI collaboration.

Do you have proof this works?

The 10 patterns emerged from hands-on experimentation — a weekend with an AI agent that revealed what works and what breaks. Every pattern comes from direct experience, not theory.
